Ajitsuke Tamago (Ramen Eggs)

Description

Known as Ajitsuke Tamago or Ajitama in Japanese, Ramen Eggs are delicious as a topping on ramen or enjoyed as a snack. It consists of a soft-boiled egg marinated in a sweetened soy-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sake (or water)
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, combine all the ingredients for the marinade.
  2. Bring it to a boil and whisk it a few times to let the sugar dissolve completely. Once boiling, lower the heat and simmer for 1 minute. Turn off the heat. Set aside to cool completely.
  3. Boil eggs for 6-6.5 min
  4. Shock the eggs in iced water
  5. Peel the eggs and place in plastic bag with the marinade
  6. Refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight
  7. They will get saltier over time, so take the eggs out from the marinade after 12-24 hours depending on your preference. Enjoy within 3-4 days
